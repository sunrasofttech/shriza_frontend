Our Sourcing Origins

Shriza Naturals was founded in the foothills of Rishikesh, born from a simple realization: modern wellness products had lost touch with absolute botanical purity. Mass production had replaced organic, cold-pressed slow extractions with quick synthetic solvents.

We chose to reverse this trend. Every single ingredient we use is ethically wildcrafted and harvested by local farming cooperatives during their peak seasonal potency.
